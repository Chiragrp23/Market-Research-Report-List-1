<p><p>Process gas screw compressors are used in a variety of industries to compress gases for various applications. There are two main types: oil-free screw compressors and oil-injected screw compressors. </p><p>Oil-free screw compressors do not use lubricating oil in their compression chamber, making them suitable for applications where oil contamination is a concern. They are commonly used in industries such as pharmaceuticals, food and beverage, and electronics.</p><p>On the other hand, oil-injected screw compressors use oil as a lubricant in the compression chamber. This helps improve efficiency and reliability of the compressor. They are often used in industries such as petrochemicals, power plants, and manufacturing.</p></p>
