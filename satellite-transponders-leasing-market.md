Ka-Band operates at higher frequencies, around 26-40 GHz, enabling higher data transmission rates for applications like broadband internet services and mobile communication. C-Band operates at around 4-8 GHz and is suitable for long-distance communication due to its high resistance to rain attenuation. Other market segments comprise specialized frequency bands tailored for specific applications like military, navigation, and scientific research.</p></p>
