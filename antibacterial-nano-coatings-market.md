The market offers various types of coatings, including Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD), Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD), Atomic Layer Deposition (ALD), and other alternatives. CVD involves depositing a thin film by the reaction of vapor-phase chemicals, PVD uses physical processes like evaporation or sputtering to create a coating, while ALD ensures atomic-level deposition through self-limiting surface reactions. The "other" category comprises alternative methods that may include different deposition techniques or unique antibacterial agents. These coatings aim to provide long-lasting antibacterial protection to various surfaces.</p></p>
