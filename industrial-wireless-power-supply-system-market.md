<p><p>The Industrial Wireless Power Supply System market consists of two main types of technologies: Electro Magnetic Induction Technology and Magnetic Field Coupling Technology. The Electro Magnetic Induction Technology utilizes a magnetic field to transfer power wirelessly between a transmitting coil and a receiving coil. This technology is efficient and widely used in applications such as wireless charging pads. On the other hand, Magnetic Field Coupling Technology involves the transfer of power through a magnetic field created between a transmitting unit and a receiving unit, allowing for efficient power transmission over short distances.</p></p>
