<p><p>Assisted bath tubs market consists of three main types: walk-in tubs, sitz bath tubs, and slide-in bath tubs. Walk-in tubs are designed with a door that opens and allows the user to safely step into the tub, minimizing the risk of falls. Sitz bath tubs are specialized for therapeutic use, providing relief to the lower part of the body. Slide-in bath tubs have a sliding seat or transfer bench, enabling users with limited mobility to transfer smoothly into the tub without the need to step over the rim. These tubs serve as safe and accessible bathing solutions for individuals with mobility issues.</p></p>
