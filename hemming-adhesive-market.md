<p><p>Hemming adhesive is used in the automotive industry for joining different metal parts together. There are two types of hemming adhesive markets: one-component and two-component. One-component hemming adhesive consists of a single product that is ready to use without any additional mixing or preparation. On the other hand, two-component hemming adhesive requires the mixing of two different components before application. Both types provide strong bonding properties, but the choice between them depends on factors such as ease of use, curing time, and specific application requirements in the automotive manufacturing process.</p></p>
