<p><p>The market for biodegradable copolyesters includes two types: PBS and PHA. PBS, or polybutylene succinate, is a bio-based polyester that is widely used as a packaging material due to its good mechanical and thermal properties. It is also used in agriculture for mulching films and in the automotive industry for interior components. PHA, or polyhydroxyalkanoate, is a bioplastic produced by bacteria. It has similar properties to conventional plastics, making it suitable for various applications such as packaging, disposable items, and medical devices. Both PBS and PHA contribute to the growing market of sustainable and biodegradable materials.</p></p>
