Electronic glass allows for natural light utilization while maintaining privacy, reducing reliance on artificial lighting and improving energy conservation.</p><p>Furthermore, the automotive industry is another key driver of the electronic glass market. The growing demand for luxury and electric cars is expected to amplify the adoption of electronic glass in automobiles.</p><p>Overall, the electronic glass market is poised for significant growth in the foreseeable future.
