<p><p>The Backup Power UPS market consists of two main types: DC Power Supply and AC Power Supply. DC Power Supply refers to a backup system that converts alternating current (AC) electricity into direct current (DC) electricity for the continuous power supply during blackouts or power outages. On the other hand, AC Power Supply refers to a backup system that converts DC electricity back into AC electricity to provide uninterrupted power to essential devices or systems. These two types of backup power UPS ensure the availability of power when the main power source fails temporarily.</p></p>
