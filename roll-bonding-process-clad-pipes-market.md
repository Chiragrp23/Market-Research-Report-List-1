<p><p>Roll bonding process clad pipes are composite pipes consisting of a carbon steel pipe as the base material and a corrosion-resistant alloy as the cladding material. The cladding material is bonded to the base pipe through a roll bonding process, where both materials are heated and rolled together to form a metallurgical bond between them. This process ensures high bonding strength and excellent corrosion resistance, making these pipes suitable for various applications in industries such as oil and gas, chemical, and power generation.</p><p>The roll bonding process clad pipes market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing demand for corrosion-resistant pipes in various industries.
