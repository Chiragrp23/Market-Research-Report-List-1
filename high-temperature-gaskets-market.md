<p><p>The high temperature gaskets market includes various types of gaskets such as metallic, semi-metallic, non-metallic, and UHT liquid gaskets. Metallic gaskets are made of metals and provide excellent thermal conductivity and resistance to high temperatures. Semi-metallic gaskets combine metal and non-metal materials to offer good sealing performance. Non-metallic gaskets, on the other hand, are made of organic or inorganic materials and provide high compressibility and elasticity. UHT liquid gaskets are advanced gasketing solutions that contain heat-resistant liquid materials, which cure to form a flexible seal.</p></p>
