<p><p>Biodegradable mulch film is a type of agricultural plastic film that is used to cover the soil surface in order to suppress weed growth, conserve moisture, and control soil temperature. The market for biodegradable mulch film can be categorized into three main types: starch-based, starch blend with PLA (polylactic acid), and others. Starch-based mulch films are made primarily from plant-based starch, while starch blend with PLA films combine starch with a biodegradable polymer. The "others" category includes mulch films made from other biodegradable materials, such as polycaprolactone or polycarbonates.</p></p>
