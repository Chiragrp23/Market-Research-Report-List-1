<p><p>Nano particle-sizing equipment is used to measure and analyze the size distribution of nanoparticles. NanoSMPS (Scanning Mobility Particle Sizer) is a commonly used instrument that operates based on electrical mobility and diffusion charging principles. NanoCPC (Condensation Particle Counter) utilizes supersaturation of a working fluid to grow nanoparticles for detection. NanoElectrostaticClassifier uses an electrical field to classify nanoparticles based on their mobility. "Others" refers to different types of equipment available in the market that use various techniques such as laser diffraction, dynamic light scattering, or microscopy for nano particle sizing.</p></p>
