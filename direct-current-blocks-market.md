DC blocks are essential in solar power systems to prevent the DC voltage generated by solar panels from damaging the power grid or inverters.</p><p>Furthermore, the rise of electric vehicles (EVs) is anticipated to propel the demand for DC blocks. EV charging stations require DC blocks to convert the AC power from the grid into DC power suitable for charging the vehicle's battery.</p><p>In terms of the current outlook, the direct current blocks market is experiencing steady growth.
