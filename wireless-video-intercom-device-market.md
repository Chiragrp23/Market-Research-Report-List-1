<p><p>The wireless video intercom device market consists of three main types of devices: door stations, video intercom masters, and indoor units. Door stations are installed outside buildings and allow visitors to communicate with occupants. Video intercom masters act as the central control unit, managing and coordinating communication between door stations and indoor units. Indoor units are placed inside buildings and provide video and audio communication with the door station. These devices enable convenient and secure communication, enhancing security and accessibility in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
