<p><ul><li>Embedded</li><li>Tethered</li><li>Integrated</li></ul></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Car sharing telematics refers to the use of technologies in car-sharing services, enabling the tracking, monitoring, and management of vehicles. Embedded telematics involves the installation of hardware directly within the vehicle, allowing real-time data collection and analysis. Tethered telematics involve devices connected to vehicles through external means like smartphones or tablets. Integrated telematics refers to systems that combine multiple applications, such as GPS navigation, vehicle diagnostics, and remote control, into one platform. These market types enable car-sharing providers to efficiently track and manage their fleets, ensuring optimal usage and maintenance.</p></p>
