<p><p>Ramming pastes, also known as ramming mass, are high-quality refractory materials used in the lining of furnaces, crucibles, and other high-temperature applications. These pastes are typically composed of various raw materials such as quartz, alumina, magnesia, and graphite, which are mixed with binders to form a homogeneous paste. Ramming pastes provide excellent thermal insulation, erosion resistance, and high refractoriness, making them essential for the efficient operation of industrial furnaces.</p><p>The global ramming pastes market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. The increasing demand for ramming pastes in industries such as steel, cement, glass, and non-ferrous metals is the primary driver for market growth.
