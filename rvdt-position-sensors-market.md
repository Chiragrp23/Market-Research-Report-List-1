<p><p>RVDT (Rotary Variable Differential Transformer) Position Sensors are electromechanical devices used to measure angular displacement. They consist of a transformer with a primary winding and two secondary windings connected in a 'series-opposite' configuration. As the rotor of the sensor rotates, it changes the coupling between the primary and secondary windings, generating an output voltage proportional to the angular position.</p><p>The RVDT Position Sensors market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years.
