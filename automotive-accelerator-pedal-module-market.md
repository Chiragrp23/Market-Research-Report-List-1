The rise in global automobile production, coupled with increasing demand for electronic throttle control systems, is driving the growth of this market. This shift towards electronic throttle control systems is expected to drive the market growth in the coming years.</p><p>In addition, stringent regulations regarding vehicle emissions and fuel efficiency are pushing automakers to adopt advanced technologies that can optimize fuel consumption.
