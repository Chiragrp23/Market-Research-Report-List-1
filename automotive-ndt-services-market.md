<p><p>The Automotive NDT Services market is categorized into various types. Ultrasonic testing involves the use of high-frequency sound waves to detect internal defects. Radiography testing utilizes X-rays or gamma rays to examine the vehicle's internal structure. Magnetic particle testing employs magnets and magnetic fields to locate surface and near-surface flaws. Liquid penetrant testing involves applying a liquid dye to detect cracks and breaks. Lastly, the "Others" category encompasses additional techniques like visual inspection, eddy current testing, and acoustic emission testing. These methods aid in ensuring the safety, reliability, and longevity of automotive components.</p></p>
