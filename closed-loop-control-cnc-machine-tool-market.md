<p><p>A closed-loop control CNC machine tool is a type of computer numerical control (CNC) machine tool that utilizes feedback sensors to ensure precise control and accuracy during operation. The increasing demand for precision machining in various industries, such as aerospace, automotive, and medical, is one of the key drivers of market growth. Closed-loop control CNC machine tools offer superior accuracy, efficiency, and repeatability compared to their open-loop counterparts, making them ideal for applications that require high precision and quality.</p><p>Additionally, the integration of advanced technologies such as artificial intelligence, machine learning, and IoT in closed-loop control CNC machine tools is further driving market growth.
