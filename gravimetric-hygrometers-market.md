There are two main types of gravimetric hygrometers: pointer type and digital type. </p><p>Pointer type hygrometers have a scale with a pointer that moves to indicate the humidity level. They are simple and easy to use, but they may not provide accurate readings.</p><p>Digital type hygrometers use electronic sensors to measure humidity and display the results digitally. They are more precise and often include additional features such as temperature measurement and data logging capabilities. These hygrometers are commonly used in laboratories and industrial settings where accuracy is critical.</p></p>
