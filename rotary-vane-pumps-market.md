<p><p>Rotary vane pumps are positive-displacement pumps that consist of a cylindrical rotor with vanes attached to it. As the rotor rotates, the vanes slide against the pump's internal surface, creating chambers that trap and transport fluid. These pumps are widely used for various applications such as industrial processing, automotive, and HVAC systems.</p><p>The current outlook of the rotary vane pumps market is positive, with steady growth expected in the foreseeable future. One of the major drivers for this market is the increasing demand for these pumps in the automotive industry. With the growing automotive industry worldwide, the demand for rotary vane pumps is expected to increase significantly.</p><p>Moreover, the industrial sector also contributes to the growth of the rotary vane pumps market.
