<p><p>The clay coated recycled boxboard market encompasses two main types: single clay coated and double clay coated. Single clay coated boxboards have a layer of clay coating applied on one side, providing a smooth and glossy surface. They are commonly used for packaging and printing applications where only one side requires enhanced appearance and printability. On the other hand, double clay coated boxboards have clay coating applied on both sides, offering superior smoothness and print quality. This makes them suitable for applications requiring high-quality graphics and vibrant colors on both sides of the board.</p></p>
